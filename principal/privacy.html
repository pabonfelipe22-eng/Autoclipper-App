Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

AutoClipper respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and safeguard information when users access our platform and interact with TikTok integrations.

1. Information We Collect

When users authenticate through TikTok Login Kit or use our platform functionalities, we may collect:

  • Basic TikTok profile information.
  • TikTok user identifiers.
  • OAuth authorization tokens.
  • Uploaded video metadata.
  • Technical and device-related information.

2. TikTok API Integration

AutoClipper integrates official TikTok developer products and APIs, including:

  • Login Kit
  • Content Posting API
  • OAuth Authentication Services

These integrations are used exclusively to authenticate users and facilitate video upload and publishing workflows.

3. Permissions Requested

The platform may request the following TikTok scopes:

  • user.info.basic — Access basic TikTok account information.
  • video.upload — Upload videos to TikTok.
  • video.publish — Publish uploaded videos.

4. How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely for:

  • User authentication and account access.
  • Providing TikTok upload and publishing functionality.
  • Improving platform stability and security.
  • Monitoring API performance and integration reliability.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect user information against unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

OAuth tokens and authentication-related information are handled securely and used only for authorized TikTok API operations.

6. Data Sharing

AutoClipper does not sell, rent, or commercially distribute user personal information to third parties.

Information may only be shared when required:

  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To enforce platform security.
  • To comply with TikTok Developer Policies.

7. Third-Party Services

The platform may rely on third-party infrastructure providers, hosting services, analytics systems, or cloud platforms necessary for service functionality and security operations.

8. User Rights

Users may request:

  • Access to stored information.
  • Deletion of account-related data.
  • Revocation of TikTok authorization permissions.

9. Children's Privacy

AutoClipper is not intended for use by children under the age required by applicable laws and TikTok platform requirements.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect service improvements, security updates, or legal requirements.
