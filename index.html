About the Platform

AutoClipper is a web-based creator tool designed to streamline the process of publishing short-form video content directly to TikTok. The platform integrates with official TikTok developer technologies to enable creators to authenticate securely and automate the publishing workflow of their content.

The goal of AutoClipper is to simplify content distribution while maintaining compliance with TikTok's developer platform guidelines and privacy standards.

TikTok Integration

  • Login Kit — Allows creators to authenticate securely using their TikTok account through TikTok's official OAuth authentication system.
  • Content Posting API — Enables the platform to upload and publish videos directly to the authenticated user’s TikTok account.

Permissions Requested

  • user.info.basic — Access to basic public profile information.
  • video.upload — Allows the platform to upload videos on behalf of the creator.
  • video.publish — Allows uploaded videos to be published directly to TikTok.
